{{#_}}Guidelines{{/_}}

  • {{#_}}Anyone can post ideas.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}Eligible ideas must be for “capital” projects: physical infrastructure for public benefit, such as park improvements or new technology for schools. “Expense” projects, such as afterschool programs or expanding bus service, are not eligible.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}To add a spot, drag the map until the crosshairs are over the desired location. The Council District in which they are located will consider ideas.{{/_}}
  • {{#_}}All ideas must be submitted by October 17, 2022. Ideas submitted after October 17th will not be considered for the Cycle 12 ballot.{{/_}}
